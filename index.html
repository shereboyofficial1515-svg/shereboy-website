About Bawo

Madamedon Bawo, professionally known as SHEREBOY, is a Nigerian content creator, musician and songwriter from Warri North, Delta State. His life is a blend of strong cultural heritage, faith, resilience and a genuine love for people.

Early Life

Bawo was born on 22 April 2000 and proudly hails from Warri North, Delta State. He grew up in a close-knit family where respect, responsibility and humility were core values.

His childhood was shaped by both challenge and love, teaching him to stand firm, stay honest and never forget where he comes from.

Who He Is Today

Today, Bawo is a blogger, programmer, content creator and musician. He uses his gifts to inspire others, tell real stories and represent his community with pride.
